Monster Survivors

Dive into a relentless world where your only goal is to outlast the monstrous horde.

About the Game

In Monster Survivors, you are thrust into a vast, unforgiving landscape teeming with creatures of the night. With each passing moment, the horde grows stronger and more numerous. Your mission is simple but daunting: survive. Gather experience, choose powerful upgrades, and evolve your abilities to become an unstoppable force against the endless tide of monsters.

How to Play

  • Move: Use your mouse, arrow keys, or WASD to navigate your character around the map.
  • Attack: Your character attacks automatically! Your job is to focus on positioning and dodging enemy projectiles.
  • Level Up: Collect the glowing gems dropped by fallen enemies to gain experience.
  • Upgrade: Each time you level up, you'll be presented with a choice of new skills or upgrades. Choose wisely to build your power!

Key Features

  • Endless Waves of Monsters: Face an ever-increasing challenge that tests your limits.
  • Deep Upgrade System: Hundreds of potential combinations to tailor your character's abilities to your playstyle.
  • Minimalist Gameplay, Maximum Action: Easy to learn, but incredibly difficult to master.
  • Instant Play: No downloads, no installations. Just click and play directly in your browser.
